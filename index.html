TNC360

Redesigning fitness app TNC360 and helping them launch the app in market.

TNC360 is a Hyderabad based startup primarily focused on fitness and creating a fitness app that comprises outstanding features to enable the coaches and fitness enthusiasts to create a universe of personal help, advice, training programs, and accountability to achieve the target results.

As a UI/UX Design Development Intern at TNC360, I built the TNC360 design system and redesigned the Android/iOS app to help them launch the app in market.

Redesigning the app

The initial design of the app was very old and it lacked certain modern elements to attract the users hence a redesign was much required.

Focusing on the two primary colors on which the whole design was made, I orchestrated and redesigned the app which would be much more suitable to the young fitness enthusiasts while making sure that it doesn't complicate things for other age groups.

Building a design system

The redesign of the application required a new design system to be built and maintained which can help other team members develop new features.

Therefore I stored all the required components in such a way that most of the elements can be reused while building a new screen or feature for the application without having to build from scratch.

EventO - Event Manager App

Building an Event Manager App to solve problems of keeping track of numerous events in an organization

EventO is an event manager app where users can list, manage & participate in numerous events being conducted in an organization. It’s very easier to use and solves the problem of keeping track of numerous events.

Problem statement

There is an annual college fest ‘Zealicon’ which runs for approximately 3-4 days. In that annual fest, numerous events are taking place everyday for students to participate and enjoy and some even at the same time.

Keeping track of all these events is a mess and it’s also a stress for students to goto each and every counter to register themselves for the event. It’s also extra work for the organizers of the event to create physical booths for registrations.

Build an app so that students can checkout all the events through their smartphone and register themselves in an instant.

Fonts & Colors

Following a two color scheme, the whole app is designed.
